### 1. What is CRM?
<br>
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used by companies to manage customer information, communication, sales, and services in one place.

It helps organizations:
<br>
* Store customer details
* Track interactions
* Improve sales and support
* Build better customer relationships
<br><br>

### 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce
<br>
Companies use Salesforce because it stores customer data securely, helps manage sales and leads, automates business processes, improves customer service, provides reports and analytics. 
<br><br>

### 3. Explain the Following
<br>
Account : An Account represents a company, organization, or customer group.<br>
Example : Vishnu Institute of Technology. 
<br><br>
Contact : A Contact is an individual person related to an account. Usually we connect with an organization with this person. <br>
Example : Student. 
<br><br>
Opportunity : An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or successful conversion.<br>
Example : Student admission process

<br><br>
